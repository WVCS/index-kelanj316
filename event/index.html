    Importance of the Moon

    The moon has a few impacts on the Earth that we may have not noticed but may now be grateful for after being informed. For one, having such a large mass in our gravitational field moderates its "wobble" on its axis, making the climate much more stable. Second, it influences the tides and currents of the oceans, or the push and pull I'm sure many of us are acquainted with. Voyagers and traveleres have utilized this force ever since boats and nautical travel came into existence. Finally, it has an influence on living organisms, which you have read about above
